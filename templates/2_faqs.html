SalinWiki is a Wiki site which serves as a digital library for different dictionaries of the following Philippine languages: Bicolani, Cebuano, Ilocano, and Tagalog. So far, we've only been able to support four Philippine languages as we fully commit to provide the best online dictionary resource and a platform for a supportive community dedicated to the aforementioned languages. However, we do believe that just as other Wiki sites develop over time through user contributions, SalinWiki's mission of breaking language barriers between filipinos will be fulfilled too as we broaden our support to other Philippine languages and reach the goal of gathering multiple users to assist with our cause.

At the home page, you can search for a word by typing it in the search bar. You can also click the button with the magnifying class icon after typing your desired word to look for it.

The dictionary webpage features sort through our online dictionaries. You may browse the list of words in the following dictionaries: Bicolano, Cebuano, Ilocano, and Tagalog dictionaries.
